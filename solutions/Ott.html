Low Code OTT Platforms of the Future

Traditional OTT platforms require significant time and resources to develop and maintain, making it challenging for media and entertainment companies to keep up with the rapidly evolving market. Mobius offers a low-code OTT platform solution that enables companies to rapidly develop and deploy customized streaming services. With Mobius, media and entertainment companies can deliver immersive viewing experiences and engage with viewers across multiple devices and platforms, all while minimizing development time and costs.

Capabilities

Scalable Video Infrastructure

Our OTT platform solution provides a scalable video infrastructure that can handle high volumes of traffic without compromising on the video quality.

Personalized Viewing Experience:

Our platform allows for personalized viewing experiences based on user preferences, viewing history, and social media data.

Content Management System

Our platform provides a comprehensive content management system that enables content owners to manage their video assets, metadata, and access controls.

Monetization Tools

We offer a range of monetization tools, including subscription-based models, pay-per-view, and ad-supported models, to help content owners generate revenue from their content.

Social Media Integration

Our platform integrates with social media platforms, allowing users to share their viewing experiences, and providing content owners with new marketing channels.

Analytics and Reporting

We provide analytics and reporting tools that offer insights into user behavior, viewing habits, and engagement metrics, enabling content owners to optimize their content and improve user engagement.

Revolutionizing the OTT experience with Mobius' low-code platform

Learn how a major media and entertainment company leveraged Mobius' low-code platform to develop and launch their own OTT platform in record time. By utilizing Mobius' comprehensive solution, which includes AI-powered video processing, customizable front-end design, and robust analytics, the company was able to provide a seamless and engaging user experience. As a result, the platform saw a significant increase in viewership and revenue, solidifying their position as a leading player in the OTT space.
